RockIt Vision

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Summary: RockIt Vision uses your camera and microphone only during active AI sessions. We do not store, sell, or share your audio or video data. Sessions are processed in real time and discarded immediately.

1. About RockIt Vision

RockIt Vision ("the App") is an AI-powered live assistant that uses your device's camera and microphone to provide real-time assistance. The App is developed and operated by RockIt Web (rockit-web.com), based in Bengaluru, India.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Camera data: Video frames are captured at approximately 1 frame per second during an active session and streamed to Google's Gemini Live API for AI processing. Frames are not stored by RockIt Web.
  • Microphone audio: Your voice is captured and streamed in real time to Google's Gemini Live API during active sessions. Audio is not recorded or stored by RockIt Web.
  • Session analytics: We collect anonymised usage statistics including session duration, assistant mode selected, and language preference. No personally identifiable information is collected. IP addresses are hashed before storage.
  • Session recordings: If you choose to record and share a session using the in-app recording feature, the video file is created entirely on your device and saved to your device storage. RockIt Web never receives or stores your recordings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered real-time assistance via the Gemini Live API
  • To improve app performance and understand usage patterns (anonymised analytics only)
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • We do not sell your data to any third party

4. Third-Party Services

RockIt Vision uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini Live API — processes camera and audio data in real time to generate AI responses. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Railway (railway.app) — hosts our WebSocket proxy server that routes data between your device and the Gemini API. Data passes through but is not stored.

Audio and video data is transmitted over an encrypted WebSocket connection (WSS/TLS). We do not transmit data in plain text.

5. Data Retention

RockIt Web does not retain your camera or microphone data. Audio and video are processed in real time and are not written to any storage system operated by RockIt Web.

Anonymised session analytics (mode, language, duration, hashed IP) are retained for up to 12 months for the purpose of improving the service.

6. Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera: Required to capture video frames for the AI assistant to see and analyse your surroundings in real time.
  • Microphone: Required to capture your voice so the AI assistant can hear and respond to your questions.
  • Internet: Required to connect to the AI processing servers.

All permissions are used solely for the purpose described. You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings, though this will prevent the App from functioning.

7. Children's Privacy

RockIt Vision is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is generally no personal data to access, correct, or delete. For questions about your anonymised analytics data, please contact us.

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR. If you are in California, you have additional rights under CCPA. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Security

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS (Transport Layer Security). We implement appropriate technical measures to protect against unauthorised access to our systems.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
